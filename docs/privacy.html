Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

QA Orchestra is a local, open-source Claude Code plugin. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

QA Orchestra collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or processed
  • No analytics or telemetry is sent anywhere
  • No cookies or tracking of any kind
  • No network requests are made by the plugin itself
  • No user accounts are required

How It Works

All processing happens locally on your machine through Claude Code. QA Orchestra consists of agent definition files (Markdown) that Claude Code reads and executes. Your code, diffs, and QA output never leave your local environment through QA Orchestra.

Third-Party Services

QA Orchestra itself makes no calls to third-party services. However:

  • Claude Code (Anthropic) processes your prompts and code according to Anthropic's privacy policy
  • MCP servers you optionally configure (Chrome DevTools, GitHub, Jira, etc.) connect to their respective services under their own privacy policies

QA Orchestra has no control over and assumes no responsibility for these third-party services.
